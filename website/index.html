What makes this different.

Other tools record clicks. AgentHandover understands intent.

🎯

Strategy, not just steps

Behavioral synthesis extracts the reasoning behind your actions. Not "click here" but "target high-signal posts because..."

🗣

Your voice, per workflow

Style analysis captures formality, sentence patterns, emoji. Casual on Reddit, formal in email. Strengthens across sessions.

🔎

Semantic understanding

Vector KB powered by nomic-embed-text. "Deploy to staging" matches "push to stage environment." Not keyword matching.

👁

Visual intelligence

Optional SigLIP image embeddings let agents find visually similar screens even when text differs entirely.

🔗

Universal agent support

MCP server, Claude Code /skills, Codex AGENTS.md, OpenClaw SOPs, REST API. One observation, every format.

🔒

Your machine, your data

Local VLM. Screenshots deleted after annotation. Auto-redaction. XChaCha20 encryption. No telemetry. Ever.
